Dongfang Xu

I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). In Dec. 2022, I received my Ph.D. degree (mit Auszeichnung bestanden) in electrical engineering from Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg (FAU), Erlangen, Germany, under the supervision of Prof. Robert Schober. Previously, I received my M.Sc. degree (mit Auszeichnung bestanden) in communications and multimedia engineering from FAU in Sept. 2017 and my B.Eng. degree in communication engineering from Shandong University, Jinan, China, in Jul. 2014, respectively.
